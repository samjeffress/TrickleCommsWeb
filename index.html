Communication creating conversations

Enable great customer experiences by sending messages to your customers when you'll be ready to pickup their call. They'll love not having to listen to your call music!

Instead of sending messages to customers in a big batch that's convenient for your business, but will cause your customers to join the queue to talk to you, Trickle Communications enables you to setup your batch of messages that will get delivered across the day - giving your customer service staff the time to respond to your customers.

How can we help?

  • Trickle a batch of SMS and email over time
  • Pause and reschedule a batch
  • Schedule one off messages

Tech details

  • Easily send individual messages using the API or NServiceBus messages
  • Integrate with an existing solution to add SMS and email functionality using the API or NServiceBus messages
  • Subscribe to events to save a sent message into your CRM for tracking and get a more comprehensive view of your customers.

Built for Windows using NServiceBus and RavenDB, powered by Twilio and Mailgun.
